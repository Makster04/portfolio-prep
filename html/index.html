Short Summary

Hello, I'm Mak Trnka, a lifelong resident of Seattle with a rich blend of cultural heritage from Bosnia & Herzegovina. My ethnic background is a fascinating mix of Mediterranean and Eastern European influences, reflecting the diversity of my ancestry. In 2022, I proudly graduated from the University of Washington with a BA in Political Science & International Studies, fueled by my passion for geopolitics and world affairs. Before that, I immersed myself in the study of human societies at Bellevue College, where I earned an associate degree in Sociology. Beyond academia, I find joy in diverse pursuits. You might catch me at the gym, honing both body and mind, or shooting hoops on a basketball court. I'm an avid traveler, always eager to explore new cultures and expand my worldview. When I'm not jet-setting, I enjoy delving into immersive video game worlds, discovering new music, and indulging in the art of cinema. Family holds a special place in my heart, and I cherish moments spent with loved ones, whether it's engaging in lively discussions or simply enjoying each other's company. As an outgoing and easy-going individual, I thrive on meaningful interactions and making connections. My interests extend far beyond the personal realm; I'm deeply passionate about politics and actively engage in socio-political discourse. I find fulfillment in analyzing and comparing different perspectives, drawing from my expertise in history and film to enrich the conversation. In essence, I'm a geopolitical and political nerd, a travel enthusiast, and a fervent advocate for understanding and empathy. Whether delving into the intricacies of global affairs or simply enjoying the simple pleasures of life, I approach each endeavor with curiosity, passion, and care.
